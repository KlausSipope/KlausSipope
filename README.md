### About me 🌍

I'm a data scientist with a background in software engineering, working in agricultural development in the world. I've spent the first part of my career building tools that help field teams, researchers, and policymakers make better decisions about food systems; from geospatial fertilizer recommendation platforms to livestock asset tracking dashboards deployed for the FAO.

My path is a bit unconventional: I trained as a software engineer, shifted into data science, and ended up spending most of my time thinking about smallholder farmers, soil health, and what meaningful data infrastructure looks like in contexts where it rarely gets built.

- 🔭 Currently working on open-source tools for agricultural data systems
- 🌱 Expanding into climate-smart agriculture analytics and Canadian agri-food sector data
- 🤝 Open to collaborations with research institutes, international development orgs, and agri-tech teams
- 📄 Co-author — *Optimizing nutrient requirement in space and time for increased crop yield and income*, Nutrient Cycling in Agroecosystems, Springer (2025)
